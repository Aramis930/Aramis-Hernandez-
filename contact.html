Whenever I’m not at school I work at a local pizzeria (Full Circle) as a delivery driver. I’ve been working at Full Circle Pizza since the summer after I graduated high school in June 2016. My very first day of work was after my orientation at UNH. Since then I've learned all about what goes into keeping a busniness together.

This past summer I got the opportunity to complete my internship at the West Haven Police Department in the Identification & Crime Scene Unit. During my internship I got the opportunity to assist detectives in tasks such as processing crime scenes, more specifically in recognizing, documenting, collecting, preserving, and analyzing of physical evidence.

My experience with the West Haven Police Department was a great experience and I got to see and do a lot. Getting a hands on experience and seeing how a police department operates was really interesting. During my time there I saw bank robbery cases, And even got to go to two autopsies.
